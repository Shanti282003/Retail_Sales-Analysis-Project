# Retail Sales Analysis Project

## Overview
This project presents an end-to-end analysis of a retail sales dataset using Python. The objective is to understand business performance by analyzing sales, profit, customer segments, product categories, regional performance, and sales trends.The project follows a complete data analysis workflow, starting from data understanding and cleaning to visualization and business insights.

## Project Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze retail sales data and identify meaningful insights that can support business decision-making. The analysis focuses on understanding sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, regional trends, and the impact of discounts on profit.

## Dataset Information
- Dataset: Sample Superstore
- Records: 9,994
- Columns:21
- File Format: Excel (.xlsx)

## Key Business Insights
- Technology generated the highest sales and profit among all product categories.
- The West region recorded the highest overall sales and profitability.
- The Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
- Sales showed consistent growth between 2015 and 2018.
- November and December recorded the highest monthly sales.
- Higher discounts were associated with lower profitability.
- Correlation analysis indicated a moderate positive relationship between sales and profit.
